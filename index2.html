As the two of you embark on your journey as a married couple, I’d like to offer you my two cents (okay, I know I am nowhere close to settling down like you two, but still!) - “Celebrate and commemorate each day together as a treasured gift from heaven.” Basically, make sure you don’t get bored of each other anytime soon!
